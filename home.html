Welcome to Sency.AI

Welcome to the official documentation for Sency.AI! Sency is a leading provider of AI-powered motion tracking technology designed to enhance human movement and fitness experiences using just a smartphone camera—no extra hardware required.

What We Offer

Our SDK provides developers with advanced AI capabilities that can be easily integrated into mobile or web applications. Here are some of the key features of the Sency SDK:

  • Data-driven Personalization: Tailor user experiences with real-time interactions that increase motivation and safety.
  • Enhanced User Engagement: Improve health outcomes and boost retention and conversion rates with personalized exercise and health plans.
  • Initial Movement and Fitness Assessments: Kickstart user journeys with detailed evaluations.
  • User Progress Evaluations: Track improvements with regular assessments and provide meaningful feedback.
  • Personalized Workout Plans: Offer tailored guidance and technique corrections based on real-time data.

Getting Started

To get started with our SDK, register now for a free client account, and gain access to demo tools and your API client key. Note: You can test our models, assessments, or conduct a brief workout without writing any code by contacting us for access to our iOS demo app.

Supported Platforms

Sency’s SDK supports a variety of platforms, including:

  • iOS: Integrate with SMKitUI for a branded experience or use SMKit for a customizable solution.
  • Android: Use our smkit-ui or smkit options for seamless integration.
  • React Native: Leverage react-native-smkit-ui or react-native-smkit for cross-platform applications.
  • Flutter: Use flutter_smkit_ui for fast and flexible mobile development.
  • Other Platforms: Contact us if your platform is missing, and we’ll assist you with our plans and integration support.
